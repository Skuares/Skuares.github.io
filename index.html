Prismatic | HoloCube

Prismatic is company operating in malaysia who deals with the 3D projector making company holoCube. The primary base station for the business is situated in Belgium. Prismatic imports Holocube products to malaysia. We were given the opertunity to design the identity of Prismatic. it was a quick design response as there was only a week to come with the proposal.




The Identity


the main challange was to relate it to the product it had to carry. the holocube. we are not certain with the mechanics of the 3D projector it self but it mirrors light into open space and keeps it structured. and the image appear to be on space. the thought behind the logotype was the name it self. Prismatic, thus something Prismatic. there’s been tons of logo which represents Prismatic shape, the 3D cubic triangle. but we wanted to get out of it and relate it to holocube projection system. as in something that expands from a singular point. the end result is simiar toa cathedrical design which goes well with the malaysian culture. we infused a glitch eﬀect of the 3D technic which mixes well with the 3d space theory, the cyan and magenta color space.



The typeface


the type face is a custom font based on pcap terminal. the lines has been straightened out from the curves. there is no curves used in this design.

The typefont


pcap terminal is a free font that can be downloaded for free commercial use. thanks to the author, it is a great font to use in technological visuals. it gives you that advanced feature look to your text. its great for representing a company that sells 3D projector.

Miss Scuba International 2014

Miss Scuba international is a annual beauty pagent to be held in Malaysia in 2014. The contestants range over a wide demographic as they represent internationally.

save ocean. save the environtment. save it through the power of beauty. influence, encourage and inform about the wild beauty of the ocean.

The proposition was to create a brand identity for the pagent. a marketing communication plan was also proposed in the early pitch. unfortunately the proposition was not to the expectation of the joint board of directors combining of more than ten different companies. a hard task to please but to built materials for the identity it self.


The Identity

the identity was to be built on ahypothetical concept as it was not possible to represent the brand by the contestants nor the place nor the sponsors as there are way too many of them. a singular identity for the entire event. and it is to repeat itself years over years. the first problem was to be solved was the identity crisis of the logotype itself. it lacked elegance and the beuty of the pagent. we designed a more suitable content driven design that goes well with the slim lines of the curves and the color of the ocean. miss scuba type was changed into a custom font and the rest has been kept smilar to the existing font type to dissimilate confusion amongst the current existing audience. it was to enhance the effect of the thought of beauty itself through the fonttype.


for the representation of the pagent we used the concept of a ocean princess who decorates herself with the beuty of the ocean. one singular identity for the entire pagent. she is beautiful, loving and she dreams in the ocean.
